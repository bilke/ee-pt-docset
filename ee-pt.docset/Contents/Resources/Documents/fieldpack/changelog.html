Field Pack 2.0.1¶

Released on Apr 29, 2013

  • Fixed a PHP error when installing Field Pack 2 if Matrix is not installed.
  • Fixed a PHP error when upgrading to Field Pack 2 from P&T Field Pack 1.x.

Released on Apr 23, 2013
