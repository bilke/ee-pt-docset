Working with Assets Fields¶

Adding an Assets field to the Publish page¶

Once Assets is installed, you can add an Assets field to the Publish page by following these steps:

  1. Go to Admin → Channel Administration → Channel Fields
  2. Click on your field group
  3. Click the “Create a New Channel Field” button
  4. Give the new field a name and a label, and choose “Assets” in the Field Type dropdown
  5. Configure the Assets field to suit your needs
  6. Click the Submit button
