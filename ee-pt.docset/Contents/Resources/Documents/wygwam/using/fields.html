Working with Wygwam Fields¶

Adding an Wygwam field to the Publish page¶

Once Wygwam is installed, you can add an Wygwam field to the Publish page by following these steps:

  1. Go to Admin → Channel Administration → Channel Fields
  2. Click on your field group
  3. Click the “Create a New Channel Field” button
  4. Give the new field a name and a label, and choose “Wygwam” in the Field Type dropdown
  5. Configure the Wygwam field to suit your needs
  6. Click the Submit button
