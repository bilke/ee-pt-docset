Working with Field Pack Fields¶

Adding a Field Pack field to the Publish page¶

Once Field Pack is installed, you can add its fieldtypes to the Publish page by following these steps:

  1. Go to Admin → Channel Administration → Channel Fields
  2. Click on your field group
  3. Click the “Create a New Channel Field” button
  4. Give the new field a name and a label, and choose the appropriate fieldtype in the Field Type dropdown. All Field Pack fields will be prefixed with “Field Pack”.
  5. Click the Submit button.
