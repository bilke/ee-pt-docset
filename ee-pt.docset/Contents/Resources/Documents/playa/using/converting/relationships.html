Relationship Fields¶

Playa comes with a tool for converting Relationship fields over to it.

Note: Do not simply change your Relationship field’s Field Type setting over to “Playa”. You will lose all of your existing relationships.

Here’s how to do it:

  1. Go to Add-Ons → Fieldtypes → Playa
  2. Choose which fields you wish to convert in the “Convert Relationship fields?” setting, and click “Submit”
  3. Updated your templates to use Playa’s template tags
